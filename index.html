About us

A Supplier of Smalls Arms

WERDA HOLDINGS (or WH for short) is one of the most famous and largest weapon industrialist firms in the entire galaxy. When a swift and reliable blaster is the only thing standing between you and a vile band of pirates, this is the shop to buy from.

Werda Holdings offers one of the widest range of weapons and gear in the known galaxy. Choose from over 60 permanently available products. All of WH's products are cutting edge technology, boast an outstanding reliability as well as quality, and come with a life-time guarantee. We deal with all political sides, freelancers, companies and governments.

Our engineers believe that achieving product excellence requires rising above our competitors by developing superior designs and manufacturing them with outstanding processes and excellent materials. Although the quality is exceptional the prices still aim at the lower end of the pricing scale.

"Hokey religions and ancient weapons are no match for a good blaster at your side." Unknown Smuggler

History

Werda Holdings was founded early Year 16 as Werda Investments by the the Mandalorian trader Werda Mird. At Day 213 in Year 16 it was renamed to Werda Holdings and became publicly known outside Mandalore space when it registered as galaxy-wide operating company. A new trading organisation was born.

Formerly specialised in the trading of valued assets like ships or stocks and investments, the company changed more and more it's buisness: After signing contracts with some of the most advanced R&D companies in small arms and personal gear markets, Werda Holdings maintained several factory cities to meet the demands of customers in this niche.

Later on that same year, the company's leader was heavily wounded on an routine action to capture some bandits. Even though he tried to manage some of the buisness once he was out of the hospital and recovered, the profits of the company suffered heavily since there was no replacement for him. On Year 17, Day 78 Werda Holdings retired from Galactic Commerce Registry due to unprofitability and provided a closing-sale of the already produced goods over the final months.

Now that Werda Mird is finally recovered, the company is looking ahead and hopes to become even bigger than before. A re-registration as a galaxy-wide company hasn't happened yet, but it's just a matter of time now that profits are growing again.

Services

Trade Management

There is something you're looking to get rid of? Or there's something you're looking to buy, but you can't find anyone selling it? Hire the experienced Traders from Werda Holdings to go after it. You name us the Maximum Price you're willing to pay/Minimum Price you want to achieve for an Asset. Now you've got the two following options: Either pay us an agreed fixed Fee if we successfully manage the Trade or simply give us half of the Profit above/below the Price you named. In any case we'll charge at least 500,000 Cr.

Loans

Loans are not offered all the time and any loan maybe individual in details. Talk to us and what you are planning and you could get support!

  • Talk to Werda Mird and tell him how many credits you want to borrow (usually max. 50,000,000 at a time) and which Assets you have that you could provide as collateral and how long you think you need the credits for. We'll usually want something as collateral that is slightly more v valuable than the amount you want to borrow. But if you have a good reputation you can talk to us about anything.
  • Depending on your details we'll offer you an Interest Rate and accept or decline the collateral for the requested amount. So you can either lower the amount or offer a higher value Collateral
  • If agreed on the details you need to send the Collateral to Werda Mird. You'll still be assigned as Commander and can make regular use the assets used as collateral. As soon the collateral is received, you'll be sent the credits.
  • You need to send at least the Interest Rate every Week. You're however free to pay off some of the Money additionally to lower Interest for next week or totally pay off the debt to receive back the collateral.
  • If you fail to send the Interest Rate we'll first look to remove it from already payed off Money. If there is none, we'll unassign you and after 2 weeks, we'll sell the collateral if there is no contact from you.
